About Asaki

Unburdened mind, that is Asaki

Asaki CASACHY, founded in 2013, seeks to revive the Song-era aesthetic and cultivate a refined, modern literati lifestyle. We distill Eastern aesthetics, unite artisanship, and craft furniture and objects with traditional techniques to recreate the ideal literati dwelling.

“Asaki” is a quiet attitude and an insight from within—an impartial pursuit of the middle way. It is a subtle feeling, like the Buddha’s serene smile when holding a flower, a gentle touch akin to the literati’s musings on Zhuangzi’s dream of the butterfly.
